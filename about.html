About Us

We are passionate home cooks dedicated to sharing delicious recipes...



At Delicious Recipes, we’re more than a culinary platform – we’re a global kitchen where tradition meets innovation, and every meal becomes a story. Founded in 2023 by chef Maria Gonzalez and nutritionist James Carter, our mission stems from a shared belief that cooking is the universal language of connection.


With over 50 combined years in professional kitchens, food journalism, and dietary research, our multicultural team crafts recipes that honor authenticity while adapting to modern lifestyles.


We meticulously test each dish through three phases: first in professional kitchens for technique precision, then in home environments with varying equipment, and finally through diverse taste-test groups to ensure universal appeal.


Our collection spans 87+ cuisines, from obscure regional specialties like Armenian Harissa to viral food trends like cloud bread, always emphasizing seasonal ingredients and local substitutions. We champion sustainability through our "Zero-Waste Wednesday" series and partner with urban farming initiatives to promote farm-to-table practices.


What sets us apart is our adaptive approach: every recipe includes gluten-free, vegan, and quick-prep variations, accompanied by video tutorials shot in real home kitchens. Our food scientists work alongside chefs to balance nutrition without compromising flavor, while our cultural historians provide backstories that transform cooking into time travel – whether you’re kneading Neapolitan pizza dough or simmering Japanese ramen broth.


Beyond recipes, we’ve built a thriving community through monthly cook-alongs, where 15,000+ members worldwide simultaneously recreate dishes, sharing results through our interactive platform. For beginners, our "Kitchen GPS" system guides users through recipes with real-time troubleshooting, while advanced cooks enjoy our "Flavor Lab" experiments with molecular gastronomy techniques.


Committed to food education, we’ve donated 12,000+ cooking classes to underserved communities since 2023.


Every recipe tells three stories: the cultural roots, the science of flavors, and your personal twist. Whether you’re a college student with a microwave or a retiree exploring artisanal baking, we’re here to turn your kitchen into a canvas where ingredients become memories, one delicious bite at a time.
